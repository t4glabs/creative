ABOUT US

Aline Partners is a fair profit consulting firm committed to supporting initiatives invested in solving public problems. Our co-founders and team members have extensive experience in strategic philanthropy, programme strategy and organisational planning. With a range of skills across the not-for-profit and for-profit sectors as well as a range of functions, we bring together the ability to build a cohesive vision and execution plan for organisations. The team has worked across themes such as food, agriculture, disability, justice, urbanisation, and energy.


We at Aline Partners come with a deep appreciation that we need an ‘ecosystems’ approach for lasting social change. Developing solutions in a time with significant social change and structural uncertainties require different stakeholders across the organisation and teams within to work in alignment. Aline Partners bring consulting services and expertise in areas of organisational strategy, programme design and delivery, finance, and technology.

OUR GOALS

Our goal is to run an ethical consulting service for high-impact social projects

We will work with both for-profit and not-for-profit organisations with whom we share mission alignment

We seek to ensure diversity within the organisation and the partners that we work with

We will charge transparently and compensate fairly.

We will do pro-bono and work with a diverse range of partners within the not-for-profit ecosystem.

Our focus will be on solving problems and getting things done for organisations.
